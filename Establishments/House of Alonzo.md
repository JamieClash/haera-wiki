The House of Alonzo is a prevalent House in [[Abstract]], but despite its ties to the Abstract monarchy, is a well-established and respected organization that researches and develops mechanical devices, with members from the [[Scholars of Thought]], and primarily [[Dwarf]], [[Halfling]], [[Human]] and [[Aasimar]] members.

Before the passing of [[Juan Alonzo]], [[Queen Masja Alonzo]] was a prominent and valued council member of the House.
