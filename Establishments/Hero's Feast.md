Hero's Feast is a [[Halfling]] tavern chain primarily based in [[Hero's Hilt]], founded during [[The Classical Era]] by [[Chardine Suave]]. 

Signature dishes:
- Grilled giant chicken's leg and titan potato soup
- Deluxe steak and titan potato fries
- Cheesy lobster and prawn (coastal establishments only)
- Hero's Feast (Hero's Hilt only)
	- salads procured from regional vegetables
	- titan potato soup
	- multiple species of bird and land animals served in various techniques: grilled, fried, baked with titan potato fries
	- exotic fruits from [[Renavoir]] and [[Intrigue]].
