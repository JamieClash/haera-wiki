Fleetfoot Post is a postage and delivery service primarily based in [[Ergo]]. In collaboration with [[The School of Ego]], Fleetfoot has developed magic-assisted postage systems that can produce temporary portals for pigeon carriers using stored magic (not to be confused with the theory of [[Stored Magic]]) acquired from low-energy magic crystals. 

Fleetfoot's magical postage system has garnered great attention, and is seen as the best postage service in [[Haera]].
