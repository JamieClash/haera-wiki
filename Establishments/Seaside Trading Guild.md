> "What's yours can be mine, to exact your price divined."
> - Motto of the Seaside Trading Guild

The Seaside Trading Guild is a trading and merchant's guild based in [[Altrae]] of [[Eastern Haera]]. Though Altrae is on the eastern coast of [[Haera]], the guild is very active and prominent in [[Western Haera]], especially the coastal cities [[Faeria]] and XX. 

The Seaside Trading Guild is known to operate generously with merchants and traders in cities across Haera, and often rents guildhall spaces to other local guilds, such as local mason, leatherworker, farmer and fishermen guilds.
