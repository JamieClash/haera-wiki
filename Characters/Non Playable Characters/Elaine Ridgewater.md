Elaine Ridgewater is the current owner and leader of the [[Seaside Trading Guild]]. She is a [[Halfling]] based in [[Altrae]] and is rarely seen outside of the Seaside Trading Guildhall. 

It is said that she orchestrates some of the most influential and important trades in the city of Altrae, and may be involved in numerous dealings in nearby regions. 

Her second-in-command is [[Gor Schmidt]], who can often be seen alongside her during big trade discussions and transactions.
