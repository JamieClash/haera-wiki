Juan Alonzo was the [[Aasimar]] king of [[Abstract]] and died at age 54 during a battle near [[Landspine]]. In life, Alonzo was a strong ruler who was fair and just. He was a powerful Paladin, but lacked inventive creativity, hence did not participate in the proceedings of the [[House of Alonzo]].
