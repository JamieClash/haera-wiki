Amelia Lukeheart was a prominent and famed [[Human]] Queen of [[The Kingdom of Marcell]] during [[The Classical Era]]. Amelia passed peacefully at the age of 66, handing the crown to her son [[Vasir Lukeheart|Vasir]].

She introduced the [[Kinsguard]] to Marcell, and was the first Queen of Marcell since [[The Forgotten Times]].
