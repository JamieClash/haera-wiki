Conduit Travel Theory (CTT) is a theory based on the magical energy lingering around the mountains of [[Conduit]]. Through years of study and research, schools and magic-related groups have arrived to this theory.

CTT suggests that the lingering magical energy can be manipulated and 'bound' to certain planes, and this bond can be enhanced with the magical energy of a user. 

Warlocks have been extensively invited to Conduit for the testing of this theory, due to their inter-planar connections with their patrons, but results are neither confirming nor denying the theory, as there is currently no method to determine whether the bond has been formed.

The primary contributors to CTT are:
- warlock
- [[The School of Ego]]
- [[The Circle of Ley Lines]]
