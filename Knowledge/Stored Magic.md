Stored magic is a magical theory by [[Forbur Wilkinberry]] and [[Seradine Blackwood]] that attunement to magic items requires a "magically-tangible" manifestation of the user's soul, thus aligning one's soul to the corresponding item.

Wilkinberry and Blackwood theorised that through capturing and replicating behaviors of a user's soul, it may be possible to duplicate the effect of attunement with magical apparatus, potentially allowing for rapid attunement switching and magic item use in combat scenarios. However, the theory is currently facing opposition in [[The School of Ego]] as success in this theory could become very dangerous in the wrong hands.
