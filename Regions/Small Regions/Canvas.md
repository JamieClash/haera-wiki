Canvas is a very large forest in [[Central Haera]], bordering [[Stockold]], [[Sandspine]] and [[Split]]. It is home to many [[Elf|elves]], druids and...

Canvas revolves around an enormous lake which is said to provide healing properties if processed correctly with the correct materials.
