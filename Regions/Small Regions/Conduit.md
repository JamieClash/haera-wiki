Conduit is a group of mountains that emerge from a vast lake towards the west of [[Upper Central Haera]]. 

Schools of wizardry, sorcery, churches and druids have studied Conduit for many years, and it is widely theorised that the mountains are host to very high magical energy, and if correct calibration and an inter-planar connection is made, it may be possible to harness the energy of Conduit to travel to other planes. See more in [[Conduit Travel Theory]].
