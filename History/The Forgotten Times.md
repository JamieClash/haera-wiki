The Forgotten Times is the earliest era in the [[History of Haera]], and only fragmented knowledge of this era is known, due to extensive monster activity in many regions, making it difficult for regions of [[Haera]] to communicate with each other. It is thought that only high-ranking families and clans have access to ancestral records referencing potential events and recollections of this era. 

Though the [[Scholars of Thought]] have repeatedly attempted to acquire information about The Forgotten Times, every known family or clan have turned them down, potentially due to some of the contents presented in such records.

[[The Classical Era]] succeeds this era.
