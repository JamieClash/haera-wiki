Abstract is a kingdom consisting of smaller towns and cities in [[Eastern Haera]] that is located near [[Renavoir]]. It is home to many [[Human|humans]], [[Halfling|halflings]], and other [[Humanoid|humanoids]], and one of the last regions on [[Haera]] that is still run by a monarch.

The current monarch is [[Queen Masja Alonzo]], who has reigned for the past three years since the death of her brother [[Juan Alonzo]].
