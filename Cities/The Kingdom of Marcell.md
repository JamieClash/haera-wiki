The Kingdom of Marcell consists of numerous cities in [[Upper Central Haera]] to the west of [[Sandspine]]. It is one of the last regions on [[Haera]] that is still run by a monarch.

The current monarch is [[King Arthur Lukeheart II]], who has participated in numerous regional battles in the past.
