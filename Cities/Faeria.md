Faeria is a coastal city in [[Western Haera]] to the north of [[Lune]]. It is neighbored by a small forest, and is the largest city in [[Haera]]. It is home to [[Human|humans]], [[Halfling|halflings]], [[Elf|elves]] and other [[Humanoid|humanoids]].
